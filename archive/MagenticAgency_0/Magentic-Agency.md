# Magentic-Agency

## Overview

Magentic-Agency is a fractional ownership exchange platform that connects users with valuable resources through time-based access models.

## Platform Resources

The platform provides fractional ownership and access to:

- **Human Experts** – Professional specialists across multiple domains
- **AI Agents** – Intelligent automation and assistance tools
- **Application Software** – Mutable applications and tools
- **Trained Models** – Pre-trained AI and machine learning models
- **Cloud Compute Resources** – Scalable infrastructure and computing power

- Access to experts in **10-minute time slices**

**Closed-Community Resources**

## For Experts

### Income & Flexibility

**Reliable Income Structure**
